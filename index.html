      Guide

      How to use

      Start here
      1. Import a book

        Tap “Import a book” and choose a PDF, EPUB, TXT or MD file.

      2. Start listening

        Choose a voice and speed, then tap “Start reading”.

        Scanned PDFs use OCR automatically.

      FAQ

      I tapped read but hear nothing?

      First tap “Test voice”.

      Reading stops after locking?

      Web speech uses the phone’s system voices.

      Why is the voice list different on each device?

      Voices come from your device system and browser.

      Scan recognition is slow or inaccurate?

      The first recognition loads a model.

      What is a neural voice pack?

      A neural voice pack is an optional advanced feature.

      Where are my books and progress stored?

      Nothing is uploaded.

      How do I install it to my home screen?

      Use your browser’s install option.

      For other issues, tap “Test voice” and “Load demo book”.

      All data stays on your device · Privacy policy

      Import a file received in WeChat

      WeChat keeps chat files in its own storage, so you must save the file out first.

      Android

      1. In WeChat open the file, tap “···”, and choose “Save to phone”.
      2. Fastest: open the system Files app and share the file to iStone Reader.
      3. Alternative: tap “Import a book” and open the WeiXin folder.

      iPhone / iPad

      1. In WeChat, choose “Open with…” then “Save to Files”.
      2. Come back here and pick the file from “Recents”.

      Tip: don’t use this app inside WeChat’s built-in browser.
